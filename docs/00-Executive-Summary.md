# VineMind AI 

# Executive Summary

## Overview

VineMind AI is an intelligent vineyard irrigation decision-support platform designed to transform geospatial evapotranspiration data into actionable irrigation recommendations for vineyard managers.

Rather than functioning as a traditional Geographic Information System (GIS) dashboard, VineMind AI acts as a digital agronomic assistant that interprets satellite observations, environmental conditions, and vineyard characteristics to recommend practical irrigation actions at the vineyard-block level.

The platform has been designed specifically for the TerraClim ET-GEO Hackathon 2026 and demonstrates how Earth Observation data can be converted into operational decision support that reduces water waste while maintaining vine health and grape quality.

---

# Background

Water scarcity continues to be one of the most significant challenges facing modern viticulture.

South African vineyards operate under increasingly constrained water availability due to:

- Climate variability
- Rising temperatures
- Increasing evapotranspiration
- Water allocation restrictions
- Rising irrigation costs

Although modern vineyards have access to high-quality Earth Observation datasets, growers often lack practical tools capable of converting those datasets into irrigation decisions.

Existing tools frequently require specialist interpretation of remote sensing products, forcing managers to manually analyse vegetation indices, evapotranspiration maps, weather forecasts and growth-stage information before determining irrigation requirements.

This process is time-consuming, inconsistent and difficult to scale across multiple vineyard blocks.

---

# Problem Statement

Current irrigation management workflows are primarily data-driven rather than decision-driven.

Growers are presented with numerous independent datasets including:

- Reference Evapotranspiration (ETo)
- Actual Evapotranspiration (ETa)
- Crop Coefficient (Kc)
- NDVI
- Satellite imagery
- Phenology records
- Soil moisture observations

While these datasets are scientifically valuable, they rarely answer the operational question growers need answered every day:

> **Which vineyard blocks should I irrigate today, how much water should be applied, and why?**

Bridging this gap between scientific observation and operational decision-making is the central objective of VineMind AI.

---

# Vision

Our vision is to make vineyard irrigation as intuitive as modern GPS navigation.

Rather than expecting growers to interpret complex geospatial datasets, VineMind AI translates Earth Observation data into clear, explainable, and prioritised irrigation recommendations.

The platform is designed to augment human expertise rather than replace it, providing growers with transparent reasoning behind every recommendation while leaving the final irrigation decision under human control.

---

# Solution Overview

VineMind AI integrates multiple sources of vineyard intelligence into a single decision-support platform.

These include:

- ET-GEO evapotranspiration products
- Vineyard block boundaries
- NDVI imagery
- Crop coefficients
- Phenology records
- Soil moisture information
- Public weather forecasts

The platform processes these datasets through a recommendation engine that calculates vineyard water demand, estimates water stress, prioritises irrigation activities and produces explainable recommendations for each vineyard block.

Instead of displaying raw scientific measurements, VineMind AI provides growers with concise operational guidance such as:

- Irrigate tonight
- Delay irrigation
- Monitor closely
- No irrigation required

Each recommendation is accompanied by an explanation describing the contributing environmental factors.
